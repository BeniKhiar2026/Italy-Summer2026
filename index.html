🌸 Florence

July 23 – 24, 2026 · 1 night
  • 08:30Breakfast at Procacci — Elegant historic café famed for truffle panini since 1885. A Florence institution. procacci1885.it — 📍 Via de' Tornabuoni, 64

  • 13:30Lunch at Il Pizzaiuolo — Authentic Neapolitan pizza in Florence. Hugely popular with locals. 📍 Via de' Macci, 113
  • 15:30Brunelleschi's Dome — 463 steps to the top of the Florence Cathedral. Pre-book online ~€18 combined ticket.
  • 16:00🚗 Pick up rental car from Avis at Via Maso Finiguerra, 13. avis.com
  • 18:30Drive to Bologna (~1 hour). Dinner at Antica Trattoria del Cacciatore — historic Bolognese cuisine. 📍 Via Caduti di Casteldebole, 25, Bologna
  • 21:00Shopping at Mall Shopville Gran Reno — outlet mall near Bologna. Check closing times before leaving.
  • 22:30Drive to Best Western Air Hotel Linate (~2h 30min). Check in for the night.

💡 Book the Avis car at least 48 hours ahead to guarantee your vehicle class. Confirm pickup process as an international traveler.

✨ Florence — Bonus Suggestions

🏪 Mercato Centrale

Florence's stunning iron-and-glass market. Ground floor: fresh produce. Upper floor: gourmet food hall.

→ mercatocentrale.it

🏘 Oltrarno District

The "wrong side" of the Arno — artisan workshops, real trattorias, zero tour groups. Walk after dinner.

→ Open in Maps

⛪ San Miniato al Monte

Hilltop church with Gregorian chant at 17:30 daily. Free to enter. Sunset views are unforgettable.

→ sanminiatoalmonte.it

🍦 Gelateria dei Neri

Locals' favourite near the Airbnb. No artificial colours, enormous portions, tiny prices.

→ Via dei Neri, 9-11r

✨ Milan & Portofino — Bonus Suggestions

🎨 Brera Pinacoteca

Milan's finest art museum — Raphael, Caravaggio, Mantegna. Often overlooked vs Duomo. Worth 2 hours.

→ pinacotecabrera.org

🏰 Castello Sforzesco

Free courtyard & park (Parco Sempione). Museums inside ~€5. A peaceful Milan morning escape.

→ milanocastello.it

🏖 San Fruttuoso Abbey

Medieval abbey on a secret beach — hike or boat from Portofino. One of Italy's best hidden gems.

→ fondoambiente.it

🛍 Via Montenapoleone

Milan's legendary fashion street. Even if you don't shop, window browsing Prada, Gucci & Versace is an experience.

→ Open in Maps
